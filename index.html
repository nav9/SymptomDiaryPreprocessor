Visualize Your Health Journey

An offline-first, private application to help you find patterns in your symptom diary data. Upload your journal, clean the data, categorize entries, and discover correlations between your activities, diet, and well-being.

100% Private & Offline

All your data is processed directly in your browser. Nothing is ever uploaded to a server, ensuring your health information remains completely private.

Intelligent Grouping

Uses string similarity algorithms to find and group related entries, making it easy to tag and categorize your symptoms, foods, and activities consistently.

Powerful Analysis

Filter, search, and visualize your data chronologically. Assign colors to categories to spot trends and correlations over time with interactive charts and tables.
